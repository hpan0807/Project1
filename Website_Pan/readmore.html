Fred Moss "Water Lillies"

The Elmhurst Artists' Guild Spring Member's Show

MARCH 11 – APRIL 20, 2018

The Elmhurst Artists’ Guild Spring Member’s Show features a variety of work made by regional artists in several media. The breadth of style and media includes watercolor, ceramics, oil, acrylic, digital, collage, photographic and sculptural works. The many emerging artists from the Chicagoland area created landscapes, portraits, abstractions and other styles. The inspiration for each artist is as diverse as the spectrum of media employed.

This show presents an opportunity to meet the artists and learn first-hand about their work. An added bonus is that all purchases provide support to the artists, Guild, and Elmhurst Art Museum.
